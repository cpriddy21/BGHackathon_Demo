Into the Future

Started at Csa

Traffic Intensity Application

Created by CSA partner Green-Light Green-Light (GLGL), a traffic intensity application was developed to mitigate traffic intensity faced right here in Vette City.

Adaptive Lighting Implementation

CSA partner-produced headlights & tail lights by PolarisLights paired with a modified ECU, cameras, and sensors eliminate the need for user input with regards to lighting, automatically adjusting depending on road conditions and warning other drivers of detected hazards.

Customizable Gauge Clusters

In collaboration with CSA partner QuickClusters, the standard gauge cluster was re-worked to provide a larger array of metrics available to the user, allowing for tailor-made displays of crucial information on the track.
